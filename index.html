mozbuild


Build Firefox, Seamonkey, and Thunderbird from mercurial repositories

With '--disable-official-branding', the builds are Nightly, Seamonkey, and Thunderbird Daily.

Build release, beta, esr60/68, or central

The build defaults to x86_64 natively, with armv7_hf, aarch64, and i686 builds being cross compiled.
